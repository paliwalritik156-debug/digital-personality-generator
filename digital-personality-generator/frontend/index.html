About PersonaIQ

PersonaIQ is an AI-powered personality intelligence platform built on the scientifically validated Big Five OCEAN model — the gold standard in personality psychology used by researchers and organizations worldwide.

What is the OCEAN Model?

The Big Five personality traits — Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, and Neuroticism — represent the most comprehensive framework for understanding human personality. Unlike other personality tests, the OCEAN model is backed by decades of peer-reviewed research.
